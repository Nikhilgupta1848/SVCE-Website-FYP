About Us

Welcome to SVCE Bengaluru, a premier institution for technical education. We offer a wide range of courses in engineering, including Computer Science, Electronics, Information Science, Civil, and Mechanical Engineering. Our focus is on providing a holistic learning experience, combining theoretical knowledge with practical skills.

About SVCE

For two decades, SVCE has been providing peerless academic, administrative, and pastoral support to students hoping to succeed and beyond. From academic aspirations to the simple day-to-day challenges of living, we understand the importance of every part of the student journey.

Academic Excellence

At SVCE, we believe in empowering students with the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in their chosen fields. Our faculty members, renowned for their expertise and dedication, ensure that students receive the highest quality education.

Comprehensive Support

We understand that students face various challenges during their academic journey. Whether it's guidance on course selection, career advice, or personal support, our dedicated staff are always available to help.

Holistic Development

At SVCE, we believe in nurturing not just academic excellence but also holistic development. We provide opportunities for students to engage in extracurricular activities, sports, and community service, helping them develop into well-rounded individuals.

Student-Centric Approach

Our student-centric approach is at the heart of everything we do. We strive to create a supportive and inclusive environment where every student feels valued and supported.

Beyond Academics

SVCE is more than just an academic institution; it's a community. We foster a sense of belonging and camaraderie among students, alumni, faculty, and staff.

Future-Focused

As we look to the future, SVCE remains committed to excellence and innovation in education. We continuously adapt to the changing landscape of higher education, ensuring that our students are equipped with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the 21st century.

Courses Offering

Computer Science and Engineering

Explore the world of computing and technology with our Computer Science and Engineering department. We offer cutting-edge courses and research opportunities in areas such as data science, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence.

Electronics and Communication Engineering

Dive into the world of electronics and communication with our department. From circuit design to wireless communication, our courses cover a wide range of topics to prepare you for a successful career in this field.

Information Science and Engineering

Learn about the latest trends and technologies in information science and engineering. Our department offers hands-on experience in areas such as software development, database management, and information retrieval.

Civil Engineering

Build the future with our Civil Engineering department. From infrastructure development to environmental engineering, our courses prepare you to tackle the challenges of modern construction projects.

Mechanical Engineering

Discover the world of machines and mechanics with our Mechanical Engineering department. Our courses cover a wide range of topics, from thermodynamics to robotics, to give you a comprehensive understanding of this field.

Placements & Recruiting Companies

Our placement cell works tirelessly to ensure that our students are placed in top companies. We have a strong network of recruiting companies that regularly visit our campus for placements and internships.
