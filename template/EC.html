Electronics & Communication Engineering Recognized as a leading center for academic excellence in creating dynamic technocrats and researchers in the field, the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at SVCE nurtures students with a sound technical foundation through efficient teaching-learning processes and state–of–the–art infrastructure.

The UG and research programs highlight passing cutting-edge expertise with forward-looking pedagogy while addressing the underlying concerns linked to the domain.
