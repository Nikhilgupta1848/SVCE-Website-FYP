Welcome to Sri Venkateshwara College of Engineering

Sri Venkateshwara College of Engineering, Bangalore also known as SVCE Bangalore was established in 2001. Sri Venkateshwara College of Engineering, Bangalore is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). Sri Venkateshwara College of Engineering, Bangalore is NBA accredited and recognised by the Government of Karnataka. At the undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral levels, the Sri Venkateshwara College of Engineering, Bangalore offers 15 programmes in the B.E, M.Tech, M.B.A, and Ph.D disciplines.

The COMEDK UGET or KCET scores are used to get admission at SVCE Bangalore UG courses, while the GATE, PGET, KMAT, MAT, and CMAT scores are used to determine admission to SVCE Bangalore PG courses. A placement department at the Sri Venkateshwara College of Engineering, Bangalore is in charge of managing placements and offering internship opportunities to students. The Sri Venkateshwara College of Engineering, Bangalore also offers a variety of amenities for staff and students, including a library, medical care, and many more.

The Sri Venkateshwara College of Engineering, Bangalore is situated on Bengaluru's NH 7 near Vidyanagar, close to the airport. The distance between Bettahalsoor Railway Station and SVCE Bangalore is 5.5 kilometres, and it takes students 15 minutes to get there. Students can get to the campus from the closest bus depot, the Sir MVIT Bus Depot, which is 4.9 kilometres from SVCE Bangalore. It takes about 12 minutes. The Kempegowda International Airport Bengaluru, which is 12.8 kilometres distant and can be reached in about 18 minutes, is the closest airport to SVCE Bangalore.
